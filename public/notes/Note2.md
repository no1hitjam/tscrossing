Day 4
The canopy is too dense to see the stars, but the compass is spinning in complete circles anyway.

Met a guy by the dry creek bed. He looked like a hiker, wore the same boots as me. He asked if I had a spare match. I gave him three. When he reached out, I noticed his fingers didn't have knuckles. Just smooth, bending skin.

I walked away fast. I can hear him behind me now, snapping twigs. He’s trying to match my stride, but he keeps forgetting how many feet he’s supposed to have.
