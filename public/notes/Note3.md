RULES FOR THE NIGHT SHIFT:

If you hear a fawn crying near the southern ridge, do not check on it. We don't have deer on this side of the valley.

If the radio static starts sounding like your mother's voice, turn the dial to 0.0 and count backward from a hundred.

If you see yourself standing outside the window looking in, do not make eye contact. You are much faster than it is, but only if you run before it realizes you've seen it.
